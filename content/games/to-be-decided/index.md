Lead Evil Inc. to world domination in the global Teewurst market and destroy all communist kangaroos along the way!
Manipulate parliament and the stock market to satisfy your desires! Use your botnet to influence the media! Embrace cars, screw consumers, and establish your own dictatorship!

## Playing

*Please note: The game is best played in full screen mode on a laptop or pc.*

You can play the game [here](./play/).

## Controls

'To Be Decided' is only controlled using either a mouse or touch pad.

## Features

- **The Parliament** decides on a law every day. It will decide on the law with the highest visibility. You can increase or decrease the visibility of a law by lobbying for or against it. Lobbying costs $100. Whenever a law is passed, it affects the parliament and stock market directly.
- **The stock market** represents your success in managing Evil Inc. Every day, your money will increase by 10% of the current stock market price. Money is the primary resource for influencing parliament.
- **The botnet allows** you to directly influence the media and public opinion. By distributing malware, your botnet grows. Posting memes makes your preferred party more popular in the eyes of the public. You can farm crypto to earn more money. When you use bribery, parliament will vote in favor of your laws more often.

## Tutorial

Unfortunately, we didn't have time to create a tutorial for this game. That's why we've written this detailed text for you.

In this game, you play an AI from Evil Inc., which aims to increase global Teewurst production. The parliament votes on laws. Some laws are very good for Evil Inc., others less so. You have the option of spending $100 to lobby for a law or to slander it. If you lobby for a law, you increase its priority. If you slander it, you lower its priority. Parliament always votes on the law with the highest priority. The stock market represents how well Evil Inc. is doing. On the one hand, it is determined by chance, and on the other, by the laws that are voted on.

The bot network allows you to hijack devices to perform various automated actions. To manually expand the botnet, you can send spam emails. You can earn money with crypto mining. You can use bribes to win over representatives from other parties. By posting memes, you can change the voting behavior of the population in your favor, gradually changing the composition of parliament. Malware automatically expands your botnet.

You win the game by destroying all critics of Teewurst through parliament, establishing a dictatorship, and turning all people into Teewurst. You lose the game if the parlament enforces communism.
