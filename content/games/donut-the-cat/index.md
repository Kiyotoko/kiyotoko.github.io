It's time to go on an epic quest to slay the EVIL DRAGON that lurks in the depths of this DUNGEON. Unfortunately the 'brave' KNIGHT that was assigned to go on this quest tripped over a CAT and fell off a cliff... Now Donut, the totally UNRELATED CAT, has to take up the cumbersome chore that the INCOMPETENT KNIGHT left behind.

 So Donut decides to explore this DUNGEON and go on a silly little KILLING SPREE >:3


## Playing

You can play the game [here](./play/).

## Controls

You control the game with both the keyboard and the mouse.

- Press the number keys 1 to 9 to choose an option. (Attack-/Dialog Choices)
- Enter an area by clicking on it with the left mouse button.
- Move the map by dragging it with the middle- or right mouse button.
- Get the name of a status effect by hovering over it's icon with the mouse.
- Quit the game by pressing Q or ESC. This only works if you downloaded it!

## Classes

You can currently play as one out of three CLASSES. You can CHOOSE from the following:

- WIZARD: Your abilities largely depend on MANA! Look out that you always have enough MANA POINTS! All abilities scale in damage with INTELLIGENCE (Int).
- KNIGHT: STRENGTH (Str) is your weapon! SLAY (✨) them with your SWORD!
- CLERIC: HEAL yourself and profit from BUFFS or DEBUFFS! The first ability scales with DEXTERITY (Dex), the second with INTELLIGENCE (Int).
