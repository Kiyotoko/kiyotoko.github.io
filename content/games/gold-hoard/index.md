Gold Hoard is a puzzle game, where you must escape the dungeon and at the same time collect as much gold as possible!

## Playing

You can play the game [here](./play/).

## Controls

- ← Move left
- → Move right
- ↑ Move up
- ↓ Move down
- Y or C Pickup Item/ Open Chest
- X or V Place Item
