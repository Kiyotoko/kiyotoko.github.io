## UNO

Does this game need an introduction? I think not. Personally, I don't like Uno, because it rules are so simpel and the very few decisions you have often don't effect if you win or not. But if you are looking for a really simple cardgame that you can take everywhere, that you can play with everyone no matter their age or willigness to concentrate on a game, than this the game for you. Additionally, UNO becomes really funny if you and your playgroup starts to create your own rules. Alternativly, you can also use a modified version like "Uno No Mercy".

## Mensch ärgere Dich nicht

This is a german race game. And its the worst board game of all. You just roll dices, if you roll a six, you can move your figure to the board. If you don't roll a six, sucks to be you! Additionally, your figures and slay the figures of other players, with the only decision made – again – by the dices. This makes the game really frustrating, because if you have bad luck, you won't get any of your figures to the board, and worse, even if you manage to get a figure to the board, another play may slay them if they just have luck. In the game, nobody makes any decisions and everything depends purely on your luck.

## Flunkyball

To be honest with you, I first saw flunkyball pretty negative because it is a drinking game and I don't drink any alcohole at all. Today, I love it because if you are the only one in the gamegroup who does not drink, you will start hitting the ball much more often then your competition :-)
